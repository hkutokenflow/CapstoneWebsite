Simple Proposal

Recent decades have witnessed the booming growth of blockchain technology worldwide. There have been studies that discuss the benefits of implementing a blockchain token system in an educational environment. Our group targets to initialize a token-based ecosystem in HKU, with the aims of incentivizing student engagement in campus activities, while educating them about blockchain technology.

We will leverage experience from existing blockchain-based projects, while innovating to address the unique needs of HKU members. Ultimately, our goal is to promote collaboration and innovation, as well as enriching the overall student experience at HKU.
