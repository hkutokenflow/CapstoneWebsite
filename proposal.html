Introduction

Recent decades have witnessed the booming growth of blockchain technology worldwide. There have been studies that discuss the benefits of implementing a blockchain token system in an educational environment. Our group targets to initialize a token-based ecosystem in HKU, with the aims of incentivizing student engagement in campus activities, while educating them about blockchain technology.

We will leverage experience from existing blockchain-based projects, while innovating to address the unique needs of HKU members. Ultimately, our goal is to promote collaboration and innovation, as well as enriching the overall student experience at HKU.

✓
Incentivize student engagement in campus activities
✓
Educate students about blockchain technology
✓
Create a vibrant token-based ecosystem within HKU
Background

Inspired by the MIT bitcoin experiment and NTUST smart campus project, our group hopes to leverage existing blockchain technology and construct a token-based economy within HKU.

The relevant studies have shown the rewarding mechanism could incentivize student engagement in campus activities. We also aim to raise students' awareness about blockchain technology through this project.

✓
Shortcomings of the token system: Will consider negative effects and potential discouragement for students
✓
Added value: Questionnaires may be necessary to gather student feedback
✓
System justification: Carefully consider the reasons why we need to build this system
Brief Literature Review

Our project is informed by several successful implementations of blockchain technology in educational settings:

✓
NTUST smart campus project: Integrated blockchain technology for campus services
✓
MIT bitcoin experiment: Distribution of Bitcoin to students to promote adoption
✓
Smiley coin experiment: Educational cryptocurrency implementation
✓
HKUST e-HKD pilot programme: Local implementation of digital currency

We will provide a comprehensive summary and analysis of token system implementations, considering both advantages and shortcomings to inform our approach.

Objectives

Our primary objective is to initiate a token-based ecosystem in HKU that incentivizes student engagement in campus activities and educates them about blockchain technologies. For safety concerns, we will not associate HKU tokens with real fiat currency or digital currency.

✓
Club and activities reward system to encourage participation
✓
Uprint: Combine tokens with printing quota system
✓
Integration with canteen services and HKU gift store
✓
Token-based tickets to concerts and other campus events
✓
Student services and products marketplace (second-hand goods, tutoring, etc.)
Method

We will implement a private blockchain on the Ethereum platform, which offers robust smart contract functionality and a well-established development ecosystem.

✓
Private Ethereum blockchain for security and control
✓
User-friendly UI System (Website and/or Android App)
✓
Smart contract development for token management

Our technology stack will include comprehensive details on architecture design and functional specifications for the token system platform. We will justify our choice of Ethereum over other platforms through literature review and technical analysis.
